Hide personal apps during Zoom/Teams screen-share in 60 seconds

Privacy & Productivity

The safest sharing choice

  • Share a specific window, not your whole screen. In Zoom: Share Screen → select the app window (or Advanced → Portion of Screen to frame just an area). In Teams: Share → Window. This avoids exposing other apps.

60-second presenter prep (macOS Sequoia)

  1. Hide desktop items instantly: System Settings → Desktop & Dock → Desktop & Stage Manager → Show Items: Off (On Desktop).
  2. Silence notifications: open Control Center → Focus → Do Not Disturb. (You can schedule a “Presenting” Focus later.)
  3. Grant screen-share permission once if prompted (Zoom/Teams → macOS Privacy & Security → Screen Recording).

Optional polish

  • Turn on Zoom → Advanced → Portion of Screen to crop exactly what viewers see.
  • Blur your video background in Zoom so the focus stays on content.
